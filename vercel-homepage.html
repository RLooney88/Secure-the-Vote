Statement of Purpose

Secure the Vote Maryland aims to restore faith in Maryland Elections through voter accuracy.
We are a team of dedicated Maryland citizens who have organized various efforts to verify voter
validity and ensure the integrity of our official Maryland voter database.

The Mission of Secure the Vote Maryland

Secure the Vote Maryland is dedicated to protecting the citizens of Maryland by assuring transparent, lawful, and trustworthy elections. Our goal is to restore faith in our elections by identifying vulnerabilities in our election system, focusing on election integrity, voter integrity, voter accuracy, and voter validity. We work to hold the Board of Election Officials accountable to their affirmative duty to maintain accurate voter rolls. Our tireless efforts have yielded evidence which demands remedies including updating, fixing, and eliminating processes that created these vulnerabilities we have identified. Our goal is to hold the State Board of Elections accountable to remedying these vulnerabilities in the system in advance of the 2026 Primary and General Election.

The right of citizens to freely choose our representatives guarantees that our government upholds our will and serves by consent. Our life, liberty, and pursuit of happiness are secured by the law. Secure the Vote Maryland is particularly committed to ensuring fidelity to the law as it relates to elections and voting. Secure the Vote Maryland is ready to guard the validity of elections through public outreach, education, and litigation.
